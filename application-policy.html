Application Policy

How applications are reviewed and processed

TalentBridge HR is designed to support direct candidate applications and structured HR review without hidden steps or unclear process changes.

What candidates should expect

  • Applications are submitted directly through the platform using the candidate's registered profile and resume.
  • Each role is reviewed based on relevant experience, document completeness, and role fit.
  • Hiring teams may contact candidates by phone or email for screening, interview scheduling, or document clarification.
  • Incomplete, duplicate, or misleading applications may be closed without progression.

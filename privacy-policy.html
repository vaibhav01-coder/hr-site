Privacy Policy

How candidate and recruitment data is handled

We collect only the information required to support registration, applications, hiring review, and follow-up communication inside the platform.

Data use principles

  • We collect personal, professional, and resume information submitted during registration and application flows.
  • Application data is used only for recruitment operations, status updates, and communication between candidates and hiring teams.
  • Uploaded resumes are stored in platform-connected storage for authorized hiring review.
  • Candidates may request access, correction, or deletion of their information subject to operational and legal requirements.

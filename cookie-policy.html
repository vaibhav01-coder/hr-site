How session and experience cookies are used

TalentBridge HR uses limited browser storage and session support to maintain sign-in state and keep the application workflow usable.

Cookie usage summary

  • Session-related cookies or browser storage are used to support authentication state and basic frontend workflow continuity.
  • No advertising, remarketing, or unrelated third-party tracking cookies are required for the hiring experience.
  • Users can clear cookies and local browser storage from their browser settings at any time.

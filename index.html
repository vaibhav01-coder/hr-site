Hiring Process

How the workflow moves from job post to hiring decision

The experience is designed to feel straightforward for candidates and operationally useful for HR teams.

01

Publish and present the role

HR creates a job post with location, compensation, department, skills, and role summary so candidates can assess fit quickly.

02

Collect complete candidate profiles

Registration captures contact details, qualification, experience, skills, and resume in a format that is easier to review.

03

Review applications in one workspace

Admins can open resumes, filter applicants, and update statuses without leaving the dashboard.

04

Keep progress visible

Candidates can return to their dashboard to check whether an application is under review, shortlisted, hired, or closed.
